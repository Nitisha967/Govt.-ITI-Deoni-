Electronics & Hardware Trade

Course Overview

Electronics Mechanic is a 2-year ITI course under NCVT. It provides training in electronic components, circuit design, troubleshooting, maintenance, and repair of electronic devices and systems.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To develop skilled technicians in electronics and communication fields.

Mission

  • Provide practical electronics training
  • Develop troubleshooting and repair skills
  • Encourage innovation and technical knowledge

Course Details

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: 10th Pass
  • Age: 14+
  • Certification: NCVT

Syllabus

  • Basic Electronics
  • Electronic Components & Circuits
  • Soldering & PCB Design
  • Testing & Measuring Instruments
  • Digital Electronics
  • Communication Systems

Career Opportunities

  • Electronics Technician
  • Service Engineer
  • Repair Technician
  • Hardware Engineer (Junior Level)
