ITI Admission Process

Step by Step Guide

1. Eligibility

Student must pass 10th class. Age minimum 14 years.

2. Online Registration

Visit official ITI admission website and create account.

3. Fill Application Form

Enter personal details, education details and select trade.

4. Upload Documents

Upload marksheet, photo, Aadhar card and certificates.

5. Merit List

Admission is based on 10th marks. Merit list will be released.

6. Seat Allotment

College and trade will be allotted in CAP rounds.

7. Admission Confirmation

Visit ITI, submit documents and pay fees.
