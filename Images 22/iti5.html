Garment & Fashion Design Trade

Course Overview

Dress Making is an ITI trade focused on garment designing, stitching, cutting, pattern making, and fashion basics. It helps students build skills for tailoring and fashion industry careers.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To develop skilled professionals in garment and fashion design.

Mission

  • Provide practical tailoring and design training
  • Develop creativity and fashion sense
  • Encourage self-employment in tailoring business

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Eligibility: 8th / 10th Pass
  • Age: 14+
  • Certification: NCVT

Syllabus

  • Basic Sewing Techniques
  • Pattern Making
  • Fabric Cutting
  • Garment Stitching
  • Embroidery & Design
  • Machine Handling

Career Opportunities

  • Tailor
  • Fashion Designer (Basic Level)
  • Boutique Worker
  • Self-employed Designer
