Mechanical Trade Training

Course Overview

Fitter is a 2-year ITI course under NCVT. It provides training in fitting, assembling, machining, measuring, and maintenance of mechanical components used in industries.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To develop skilled technicians for mechanical and industrial sectors.

Mission

  • Provide practical mechanical training
  • Develop accuracy and precision skills
  • Encourage industrial and self-employment skills

Course Details

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: 10th Pass
  • Age: 14+
  • Certification: NCVT

Syllabus

  • Bench Work & Fitting
  • Measuring Tools (Vernier, Micrometer)
  • Drilling & Tapping
  • Lathe Machine Basics
  • Grinding & Finishing
  • Workshop Safety

Career Opportunities

  • Fitter
  • Mechanical Technician
  • Maintenance Technician
  • Production Worker
