Electrical Trade Training

Course Overview

Electrician is a 2-year ITI course under NCVT. It provides knowledge about electrical wiring, machines, safety, installation, and maintenance of electrical systems.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To develop skilled electricians for industrial and domestic sectors.

Mission

  • Provide electrical and practical training
  • Develop safety awareness
  • Encourage technical skills and self-employment

Course Details

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: 10th Pass
  • Age: 14+
  • Certification: NCVT

Syllabus

  • Electrical Wiring
  • AC & DC Machines
  • Transformer
  • Motor Winding
  • Electrical Safety
  • Basic Electronics

Career Opportunities

  • Electrician
  • Wireman
  • Maintenance Technician
  • Industrial Electrician
