Painting & Surface Finishing Trade

Course Overview

Painter is an ITI trade that provides training in surface preparation, painting techniques, color mixing, polishing, and finishing work used in buildings, vehicles, and industries.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To develop skilled painters for construction and industrial sectors.

Mission

  • Provide practical painting training
  • Develop creativity and finishing skills
  • Encourage self-employment and entrepreneurship

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Eligibility: 10th Pass
  • Age: 14+
  • Certification: NCVT

Syllabus

  • Surface Preparation
  • Wall Painting Techniques
  • Color Mixing
  • Spray Painting
  • Polishing & Finishing
  • Safety Practices

Career Opportunities

  • Painter
  • Spray Painter
  • Polishing Worker
  • Self-employed Contractor
